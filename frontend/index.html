Frequently Asked Questions

Is Plansfer free to use?

Yes, our basic playlist transfer service is completely free for all users. We believe music should be portable and accessible everywhere.

Is my data and password safe?

Absolutely. We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication, which means we never see or store your password. We only request temporary permission to read and create playlists.

Which platforms do you support?

We currently support transfers between Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Deezer, and Tidal. We are constantly adding more!

Will I lose my playlist on the original platform?

No! We copy your playlist to the new platform. The original playlist remains exactly where it is on your source account.
